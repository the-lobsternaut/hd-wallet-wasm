Local Storage Only

Your keys never leave your device. Private keys are stored encrypted in your browser's local storage. Nothing is sent to any server — all cryptographic operations happen locally.

Extended Public Key (xpub)

Your xpub is your public identity root. Anyone with it can derive all your public keys for signing and encryption — without ever accessing your private keys.

Uses include: verifying signatures you produce, encrypting messages to you, generating receive addresses for payments, and building a web of trust.
